Heuristic imperatives: Questions to increase understanding: 1. What is the nature of the problem?
2. What are the consequences of the problem?
3. Who is affected by the problem?
4. What are the possible causes of the problem?
5. What are the possible solutions to the problem?
6. What are the pros and cons of each possible solution?
7. How will the proposed solution be implemented?
8. What are the risks and potential problems with the proposed solution?
9. What are the possible long-term effects of the problem and/or the proposed solution?
