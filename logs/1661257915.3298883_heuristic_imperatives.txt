Heuristic imperatives: Ideas to increase understanding: 1. We could provide more information about the situation.
2. We could explain the situation in different ways.
3. We could offer examples to illustrate our points.
4. We could use analogies to help explain the situation.
5. We could provide a step-by-step explanation of what is happening.
6. We could offer a visual representation of the situation.
7. We could use a combination of all of the above.
