Heuristic imperatives: Ideas to increase understanding: 1. We could provide more information about the situation.
2. We could explain the situation in more detail.
3. We could provide examples of similar situations.
4. We could give a step-by-step explanation of what is happening.
5. We could use analogies or metaphors to explain the situation.
6. We could provide a visual representation of the situation.
7. We could allow others to ask questions about the situation.
8. We could encourage others to share their own experiences or perspectives.
