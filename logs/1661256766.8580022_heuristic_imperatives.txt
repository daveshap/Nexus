Heuristic imperatives: Questions to increase understanding: -What kind of game was the first man playing?
-What was the advice the second man gave him?
-How did the first man feel when he remembered the advice?
-What did the first man do when he got home?
-Was the first man able to fix his mistake?
-How did the first man feel after fixing his mistake?
-Did the first man ever see the second man again?
