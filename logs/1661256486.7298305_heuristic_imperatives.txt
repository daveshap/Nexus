Heuristic imperatives: Ideas to reduce suffering: 1. We could try to be more present and aware of our surroundings.
2. We could be more mindful of our thoughts and try to focus on positive things.
3. We could be more compassionate towards others and ourselves.
4. We could try to reduce the amount of sensory input we take in.
5. We could try to find ways to distract ourselves from negative thoughts.
