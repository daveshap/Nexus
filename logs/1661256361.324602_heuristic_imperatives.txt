Heuristic imperatives: Questions to increase understanding: A person wakes up in a hospital bed with no memory of how they got there.

1) How long have I been here?
2) What is the last thing I remember?
3) How did I get here?
4) Who found me?
5) Who are the doctors?
6) What is wrong with me?
7) How will I get better?
8) What happens if I don't get better?
9) What is this place?
10) Have I been here before?
