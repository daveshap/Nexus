Heuristic imperatives: Ideas to increase prosperity: 1. We could create more opportunities for people to interact with each other and with nature.
2. We could create more opportunities for people to engage in meaningful work that they enjoy.
3. We could provide more support for people to care for their physical and mental health.
4. We could create more opportunities for people to connect with their community and feel a sense of belonging.
5. We could increase access to education and information so people can make informed decisions about their lives.
