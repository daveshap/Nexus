Heuristic imperatives: Questions to increase understanding: 1. What is the name of the company?
2. What is the name of the product?
3. What are the ingredients in the product?
4. How is the product used?
5. What are the benefits of the product?
6. Are there any side effects associated with the product?
7. Who should use the product?
8. How often should the product be used?
9. Where can the product be purchased?
10. Is the product backed by a satisfaction guarantee?
