Heuristic imperatives: Questions to increase understanding: -What was the advice the second man gave the first?
-Did the first man take the advice?
-What was the first man's mistake in the game?
-Why was the first man trying to figure out his mistake?
-What was the first man's reaction to the advice?
-What was the first man's reaction to realizing his mistake?
-What would have happened if the first man hadn't remembered the advice?
