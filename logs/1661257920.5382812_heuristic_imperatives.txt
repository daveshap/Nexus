Heuristic imperatives: Questions to increase understanding: You are a new hire at a small company. You have been working there for two weeks and have been assigned to work on a project with a team of three other people. One of your team members, Sarah, seems to be very bossy and always has to be in control. She is always telling you what to do and how to do it. What should you do?

1. Have you talked to Sarah about her behavior?
2. Do you think her behavior is intentional?
3. What do the other team members think about Sarah's behavior?
4. Have you talked to your manager about Sarah's behavior?
5. What do you think is the best way to handle this situation?
